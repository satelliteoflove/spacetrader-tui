# Skills & Mercenaries Specification

## Overview
Skills influence player effectiveness in piloting, combat, trading, and engineering. Mercenaries can be hired to boost lacking skills.

## Skills
- Pilot: Fleeing, dodging, pursuit
- Fighter: Combat accuracy
- Trader: Prices for goods, ships, equipment (up to 10% discount)
- Engineer: Ship/shield repair, weapon effectiveness

## Player & Mercenaries
- Player has base skill values (randomized or chosen at start)
- Mercenaries available for hire at some spaceports
- Mercenaries have individual skill profiles
- Best skill (player or mercenary) is used for relevant checks

## Hiring & Payment
- Mercenaries require daily wage (paid on warp)
- If unpaid, mercenaries leave
- Can only hire up to available crew quarters

## Skill Effects
- Piloting: Fleeing/escaping, pursuit, evasion
- Fighting: Weapon hit chance
- Trading: Price negotiation
- Engineering: Repairs, shield recharge, weapon bonus
- Gadgets can boost skills (navigation, targeting, auto-repair)

## Persistent State
- Hired mercenaries, skills, and wages persist across saves/loads
