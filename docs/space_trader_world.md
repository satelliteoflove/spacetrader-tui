# World Generation & Economy Specification

## Overview
The galaxy is a procedurally generated set of star systems, each with unique properties affecting gameplay and economy.

## Star System Attributes
- Name (random or from list)
- Tech level (Pre-agricultural, Agricultural, Medieval, Renaissance, Early Industrial, Industrial, Post-industrial, Hi-tech)
- Political system (Anarchy, Democracy, Theocracy, etc.)
- Special resources/events (e.g., mineral-rich, drought, war)
- Market (goods available, prices)

## Tech Levels
- Influence available goods, equipment, ship types
- Affect price ranges for goods

## Political Systems
- Influence encounter rates (police, pirates, traders)
- Affect legality of goods, bribery, fines
- Impact market prices

## Special Events
- Temporary modifiers to prices and encounters (e.g., war, famine, boredom)
- Communicated via in-game news

## Market Dynamics
- Prices fluctuate daily (random, event-driven, skill-driven)
- Stock replenishes over time
- Persistent across saves/loads
